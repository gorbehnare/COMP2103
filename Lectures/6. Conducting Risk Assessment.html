6. Conducting Risk Assessment

Risk Assessment Process

Key activities and guidelines are provided in Chapter 3 of NIST SP 800-30r1, Guide for Conducting Risk Assessments.
Additional resources are provided in the appendix of the same document as well. These include the tabulated list of typical items for each risk factor (Appendix D to H), as well as guidelines on how to write a risk assessment report (Appendix K).

  • Step 1: Prepare
    • Purpose
    • Scope
    • Assumptions and constraints
    • Sources of information
    • Risk model and analytic approaches
  • Step 2: Conduct
    • Threat sources + Threat Events
    • Vulnerabilities
    • Likelihood
    • Impact
    • Risk
  • Step 3: Communicate (Document the results, Create and publish/share reports)
  • Step 4: maintain (Monitor for any changes, re-assess)
